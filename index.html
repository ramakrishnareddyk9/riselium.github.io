Home Buying Made Simple
Buyer's Guide
Owning a home is a dream for many — and one of the most important steps towards fulfilling that dream is securing a home loan. Here's a simple guide with frequently asked questions.
FAQ'S
Are you eligible for a home loan? ▼

If you answer yes to the following, obtaining a home loan should be smooth:

  • You are employed with a reputed firm, government organization, or have a stable professional income.
  • You maintain a clean financial record and good savings habits.
  • Your income is sufficient to comfortably repay the loan through monthly installments (EMIs).

Choosing a reputed developer like Riselium Constructions also simplifies the process, as our projects are typically pre-approved by leading banks.

Home Loan Process — Step by Step ▼

A home loan is a long-term partnership between you and your bank — often spanning over a decade. The process typically involves:

  1. Shortlisting banks or financial institutions
  2. Submitting the application form
  3. Personal Discussion with the banker
  4. Field Investigation
  5. Credit appraisal and loan sanction
  6. Issuance of the Offer Letter
  7. Submission of property documents for legal verification
  8. Technical evaluation of the property
  9. Property valuation
  10. Registration of the property
  11. Signing of agreements and submission of post-dated cheques
  12. Disbursement of the loan
Documents Required ▼

Banks require several documents to evaluate your eligibility:

Proof of Income:

  • Last three years' Income Tax Returns or Form 16
  • Bank account statement for the past six months
  • Salary slips or income certificates

Age and Address Proof: Any one of — SSC certificate, driving license, passport, ration card, PAN card, or voter ID card.

Identity Proof: PAN card, passport, or driving license.

Employer or Business Details: If self-employed, provide details of your business activity, financial performance, clients, and turnover.

Key Evaluation Factors ▼

Banks assess multiple aspects before approving your loan:

  • Financial history: Regular savings, healthy account balances, and minimal cheque bounces strengthen your case.
  • Existing obligations: Share details of any current loans or EMIs.
  • Clean records: Transparency helps — incomplete disclosures often delay approval.
Processing Fee ▼

Banks charge a processing fee to evaluate and process your loan application. This typically ranges between 0.25% and 0.50% of the loan amount or a fixed sum (₹5,000–₹10,000).

Some banks may offer partial or full waivers during special campaigns.

Personal Discussion and Verification ▼

Once the documents are reviewed, banks may invite you for a brief personal discussion to clarify details. They will also independently verify your address, place of work, and references through specialized agencies.

Loan Sanction and Offer Letter ▼

If approved, you will receive a Home Loan Sanction Letter outlining key terms:

  • Loan Amount
  • Interest rate (fixed or floating)
  • Reference rate and revision frequency
  • Tenure and repayment schedule

Read the offer carefully before signing the acceptance copy.

Legal and Technical Scrutiny ▼

Before disbursal, banks will conduct:

  • Legal verification: To confirm the authenticity of property documents.
  • Technical evaluation: To assess construction quality, layout, and compliance with local authority approvals.

If the property is under construction, the bank's technical team verifies progress and ensures it matches the builder's payment schedule.

Registration and Disbursement ▼

After approvals, you will:

  • Register the property at the sub-registrar office by paying stamp duty and registration charges
  • Submit original property documents to the bank
  • Sign the loan agreement and provide post-dated cheques or set up ECS/auto-debit
  • Receive loan disbursement — either in full (ready property) or in stages (under construction)

Opting for a Riselium Constructions project gives you a distinct advantage — most of our developments are pre-approved by major banks, ensuring faster loan processing, transparent documentation, and smoother disbursal.
